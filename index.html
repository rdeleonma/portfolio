about me

I put my all into everything I do.

I got into web design after realizing that in order to find a career I would enjoy I needed to find something that I could apply my diverse and quite quirky skillset to. This led me to a field where my highly inquisitive nature, attention to detail, and perserverance would come in very handy. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I'd found something that gave me the freedom, versatility, and satisfaction I was looking for in my work and I never looked back.

I am looking forward to creating something great with you!
